Assessment11

Q1: Name 2 typical real-world applications for a “Loft” feature.

(為“Loft”功能命名2個典型的實際應用程序。)

A1: Fan blades, aircraft wings, propellor, other aerodynamic applications, etc.

(風扇葉片，飛機機翼，螺旋槳，其他空氣動力學應用等。)

Q2: Which sketch tool is the best for tracing over curvy lines?

(哪種草圖工具最適合追踪曲線？)

A2: Spline.

(仿樣。)

Q3: What does it mean to “emboss” text onto the surface? 

(將文字“浮雕”到表面上意味著什麼？)

A3: It is when the text is either raised, or sunken, into the surface of a part

(當文字被抬起或凹陷進入零件表面時)

Q4: What is a common application of a “Helix” feature? 

(“Helix”功能的常見應用是什麼？)

A4: To model a spring, or a threaded surface.

(模擬彈簧或螺紋表面。)

Q5: What does it mean to “Branch and Merge” a design in Onshape? 

(在Onshape中“分支和合併”設計意味著什麼？)

A5: Generally speaking, “branching” and “merging” are tools used in concurrent design,
where design variations are investigated at the same time. A “Branch” represents one of
the several design options, and a “Merge” is when two design options are combined,
where the result has the design features of both options.

(一般來說，“分支”和“合併”是並發設計中使用的工具，同時調查設計變化的地方。 “分支”代表其中之一幾個設計選項和“合併”是兩個設計選項相結合，結果具有兩個選項的設計特徵。)
